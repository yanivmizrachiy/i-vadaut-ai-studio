## Source File Overview

Source file: `אי וודאות.pdf`  
Total pages: 62  
Subject domain: uncertainty, statistics, probability, data literacy, critical interpretation of representations.

The file is a pedagogical sequence for teaching uncertainty through:

- collecting and organizing data;
- frequency tables;
- bar charts;
- pie charts;
- relative frequency;
- percentages;
- intuitive probability;
- measures of center and range;
- misleading graphs;
- interpolation;
- extrapolation;
- integrating information from multiple sources.

## Core Teaching Progression

1. Grade 7 — Basic statistics: data collection, classification, frequency, tables, bar charts, pie charts, and representation conversion.
2. Grade 7 — Relative frequency: comparing groups fairly by moving from raw counts to ratios, fractions, decimals, and percentages.
3. Grade 7 — Intuitive probability: probability as a measure of likelihood and as an interpretation of relative frequency.
4. Grade 8 — Measures of center and spread: range, mode, median, mean, sensitivity to extreme values, and choosing a representative measure.
5. Grade 8 — Probability: basic probability, complementary events, symmetry, fairness, and real-life probability interpretation.
6. Data literacy and critical thinking: reading, questioning, and evaluating graphs, including misleading visual representations.
7. Interpolation and extrapolation: estimating missing values inside or outside known data ranges, while understanding uncertainty.
8. Multiple information sources: combining tables, graphs, diagrams, and written information to answer real-world questions.
